Common questions

Everything you might want to know before you start.

Is Aether free to use?
Yes — fully free with no account required. We may add an optional premium tier in the future for higher concurrent streams, but the core product stays free.
Do I need to install anything?
No. Aether runs entirely in your browser. Open the site, paste a link, watch or download. Works on phones, tablets, and laptops.
Where are my downloads saved?
Directly to your device's default download folder. We never store or proxy your videos through our servers in a way that retains them — sessions are ephemeral.
Why is the file smaller than the original?
If you picked a lower quality (720p or 360p), the file is compressed accordingly. Pick 1080p for the original source quality where available.
What formats does Aether support?
MP4, MKV, MOV, AVI, and HLS streams (.m3u8). Audio in AAC, MP3, and Opus. If the source is something exotic, we'll still try to transcode on the fly.
I own the rights to a file — how do I report misuse?
Email our DMCA contact (in the footer). We respond within 24 hours and honor takedown notices promptly.
